Darjeeling Mall

Mall is the town center or the social center of Darjeeling. This is where some of the old and heritage stores are lined up on one side, and there is open view on the other side that is fringed by Pine trees.


Puttabong Tea Estate

Originally known as Tukvar Tea Estate, Puttabong Tea Estate is considered to be the oldest tea estate in Darjeeling. Based at an altitude of 1500 to 6500 ft., the sprawling beautiful tea garden also overlooks the snow-capped Kanchenjunga Peak. The estate uses sustainable products and techniques like vermicompost, cattle farming, and liquid fertilizers. It produces some of the best quality tea in the world.


Tiger Hill

Laying at an altitude of 2590 meters and a distance of 13 Kilometers from the Darjeeling, Tiger Hill is most famous for its splended sights of sunnrise from where you can see the peaks of Kanchenjunga illuminate before the sun is seen at lower elevations. The magnificent view of snow-capped mountains sitting amidst cotton clouds attracts tourist to Tiger Hill form all over the country.


Orange Garden

One of the most untouched parts of the Darjeeling Himalayan foothills, Sittong & Shelphu Hills are treasure trove yet to be discovered. There are so many unknown villages with Orange Orchard in North Bengal-Mirik,Sittong,Shelphu & Mangwa are one of them. Every house in Shittiong has a garden and every garden has Orange trees. The whole village turns Orange during winter.
